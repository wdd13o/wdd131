Project Site Plan

Site Name

JayCode

This name represents an online learning platform focused on providing free and paid courses in various in-demand domains such as technology, business, and arts. The name "JayCode" reflects the idea of coding and learning, which is central to the platform's mission.

Optional domain availability: https://wdd13o.github.io/wdd131/project/index.html

Site Purpose

The purpose of JayCode is to provide a comprehensive online learning platform where users can access free and paid courses, earn certificates, and enhance their skills in various domains. The site will offer:

  • A wide range of courses in technology, business, arts, and more.
  • Interactive learning experiences with quizzes, projects, and live sessions.
  • Certificates for completed courses to help users showcase their skills.

Scenarios

Scenarios represent the questions or needs of the target audience. Here are two scenarios for JayCode:

  1. What free courses are available for beginners in programming? - A visitor wants to start learning programming and is looking for beginner-friendly courses.
  2. How can I get a certificate for completing a course? - A visitor wants to know the process of earning a certificate after completing a course.

Color Schema

The color schema for JayCode is designed to be vibrant and professional. Below are the primary colors and their usage:

#fb873f
(Primary)
#333
(Text)
#f9f9f9
(Background)

Usage:

  • #fb873f: Used for headings, buttons, and accents to create a vibrant and engaging look.
  • #333: Used for body text to ensure readability.
  • #f9f9f9: Used as the background color for a clean and professional appearance.

Typography

The typography for JayCode is clean and modern, ensuring readability and a professional look. The following fonts will be used:

  • Headings: "Nunito", sans-serif - A modern and clean font for headings.
  • Body Text: "Heebo", sans-serif - A readable and professional font for body text.

These fonts are selected to provide a consistent and visually appealing experience across the site.
